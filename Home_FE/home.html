WHAT IS ECHOVisit?

ECHOVisit is a secure portal used by both healthcare providers and patients. It allows healthcare providers to record, store, and share information with their patients. ECHOVisit allows patients to play an active role in their health and wellbeing because they are able to have access to their test results and ask relevant questions as needed.
